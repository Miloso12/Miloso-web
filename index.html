My project link

My footstep power generation project using pinion and rack gears is a mechanical approach to harvesting energy. When a person steps on the system, the rack moves linearly, engaging the pinion gear to rotate. This rotational motion can then drive a generator to produce electricity. This method is efficient in areas with heavy foot traffic, such as sidewalks, shopping malls, and public transport stations. It’s a cost-effective and durable solution compared to piezoelectric alternatives. Are you designing it for a specific location or testing different load capacities?
